Current State

VR INDUSTRY

The VR market has seen significant growth throughout 2020 and the pandemic. Compared to the growth between 2017-2019, 2020 saw an additional 25% increase above normal. This trend continued into 2021 where we saw a continued increase in adoption of VR gaming. Many attribute this to the advancements made in technology and the increase in quality of games available.

Best Selling VR Games

Half-Life: Alyx

This game is a first person shooter developed by Valve and released in 2020.
The game was extremely well received placing itself in the top 20 games of all time aswell as becoming the best selling VR title to date.

Beat Saber

A rhythm based VR title designed to get you moving. Released by Beat Games in 2019.
Beat saber took the rhythm game and adapted it to VR perfectly, landing it as the second best selling title. With it also gaining massive continued critical success.


Boneworks

This game is a narrative adventure based title. Design and released by Stress Level Zero in 2019.
At the time of release it was one of the best VR physics simulator with a sequel set to release in 2022.

Pavlov VR

Multiplayer combat VR title with emphasis on realism. Published by Vankrupt Games and released in 2017.
Pavlov is the number one most played VR shooter with the ability to make community maps and game modes the game has been able to persist for many years.

The Future

VR INDUSTRY

The VR and AR market is set to grow dramatically through to 2025 with expected growth to be 48.8%. This expected increase is due to the VR industry entering into the phone market, with the new advancements in technology and the price of the hardware becoming cheaper and cheaper. The mainstream market will likely grow dramatically.
